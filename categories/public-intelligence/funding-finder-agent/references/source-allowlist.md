# Source Allowlist (Grants and Funding Only)

Use these official sources first. Exclude procurement/tender result types.

## Priority 1

- Funding and Tenders Portal (funding section only): `https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/`
- Horizon Europe funding pages: `https://research-and-innovation.ec.europa.eu/funding/`
- Digital Europe Programme pages: `https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/activities/digital-programme`
- LIFE Programme (climate/environment calls): `https://cinea.ec.europa.eu/programmes/life_en`
- EU Mission Climate-Neutral and Smart Cities: `https://research-and-innovation.ec.europa.eu/funding/funding-opportunities/funding-programmes-and-open-calls/horizon-europe/eu-missions-horizon-europe/climate-neutral-and-smart-cities_en`

## Priority 2

- European Innovation Council pages: `https://eic.ec.europa.eu/`
- CORDIS programme/project context: `https://cordis.europa.eu/`
- data.europa.eu datasets catalog: `https://data.europa.eu/`
- Rothschild Foundation Hanadiv Europe (grants page): `https://rothschildfoundation.eu/grants-page/`
- Rothschild Foundation Europe (collaborative fellowships): `https://rothschildfoundation.eu`
- EUJS (grants page): `https://www.eujs.org/grants`

## Filtering Rule

Drop any result that appears procurement-oriented (for example, tender notices, contract awards, contracting authority language).

Keep only opportunities that clearly represent:

- grants
- startup/innovation funding programs
- EU-backed funding support calls
